## 1. Scope

This Policy applies to products and services we provide to you through:

1. The EcoLove (愛環保) mobile application
2. Online features, customer support, and after-sales communications related to this App
3. Related electronic services we may launch in the course of our business

If a specific product, activity, or feature has its own privacy notice, supplementary terms, or terms of service, those documents take precedence for that feature where they conflict; where not specifically addressed, this Policy applies.

## 2. Principles for processing personal data

We will use reasonable endeavours to ensure that we:

1. Collect only data that is directly related, necessary, and proportionate to our services
2. Process personal data lawfully, transparently, and fairly
3. Use data only for the purposes stated or purposes directly related to them
4. Implement reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data security
5. Delete or anonymise data when it is no longer needed

## 3. Categories of personal data we may collect

Depending on the features you use, we may process the following categories of data.

### 3.1 Account and identity data

When you register, sign in, or maintain an account, we may process:

- Display name
- Email address
- Account identifiers returned by third-party sign-in
- Firebase user identifiers
- Other necessary account data returned by authentication services, where enabled in a given version

The App currently supports third-party sign-in, including:

- Sign in with Apple
- Google Sign-In

### 3.2 Address book and contact details

When you add, edit, or use addresses, we may process:

- Address labels
- Contact name
- Phone number
- Region, district, street/building, floor/flat, and similar address fields
- Whether an address is the default

### 3.3 Recycling requests and order data

When you submit a recycling request, use the recycling cart, or view orders, we may process:

- Recycling category and subcategory
- Brand, model, RAM, storage, colour
- Quantity and unit
- Item condition
- Collection method (e.g. door-to-door or mail-in)
- Appointment date and time slot
- Mailing or pickup address
- Order number, creation time, status timeline, and order history
- Attachment links and related order metadata
- Payout or points options you select, where applicable

### 3.4 Content you upload

When you use upload features, we may process:

- Images
- Photos taken with the camera
- PDF files
- File names, types, links, and other necessary metadata

### 3.5 Chat and customer support

When you use order chat or support, we may process:

- Messages you send and receive
- Message timestamps
- Records related to orders or after-sales support

### 3.6 Points, QR code, and account display

When you use account-related display features, we may process:

- User account identifiers (for QR generation)
- Points balances or related display data on rewards screens

The QR screen is intended for staff or service personnel to identify your account for points or service workflows.

### 3.7 Local preferences and cache

To improve your experience, the App may store preferences and cache locally on your device, such as:

- Language
- Font size
- Onboarding completion state
- Recycling cart drafts or items
- Store cart and favourites
- Cached user type (e.g. residential / commercial)

Such data is stored mainly on your device for faster loading and continuity.

### 3.8 Device and technical data

To keep the App running, we may process limited technical data, such as:

- Device model
- OS version
- App version
- Basic logs and error diagnostics
- Current language or region settings

Unless required by law or necessary for a specific feature, we do not collect unrelated device data without justification.

## 6. Device permissions and purposes

We do not enable sensitive permissions without your action. Permissions are usually requested when you use the related feature.

### 6.1 Camera

- Take photos of items
- Add order attachments

If you deny camera access, most features still work, but you cannot take photos in-app.

### 6.2 Photo library / media

- Choose existing images to upload

If you deny access, you cannot pick from the library; other features are unaffected.

### 6.3 Files / documents

- Select and upload PDF files

### 6.4 Clipboard (write)

- When you tap copy, copy the mailing address to the clipboard

We do not read unrelated clipboard content for this feature.

You may remove some local data by uninstalling the App, clearing app data, or removing content in relevant features.

## 10. Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes in this Policy, unless a longer period is required by law.

Generally:

1. Account data is kept while your account exists.
2. Address data is kept while you keep those records.
3. Order data is kept as needed for operations, audit, disputes, and compliance.
4. Chat and attachments are deleted or anonymised after service completion and a reasonable retention period.
5. Local cache is removed or becomes unavailable when you delete content, clear app data, or uninstall the App.

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymise related data where permitted by law and technically feasible.

Cloud and technology providers may be located outside Hong Kong; your data may be transferred, stored, or processed outside Hong Kong.

Where that occurs, we take reasonable measures to ensure a level of protection comparable to what Hong Kong law requires, such as contractual safeguards, access controls, encryption in transit, storage restrictions, and limited data access.

## 12. Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures, including:

1. Authentication and access controls
2. Data transmission protections
3. Restricted cloud access
4. Logging and error monitoring
5. Limited internal access
6. Minimising unnecessary processing

### 13.2 Correction

Request correction if data is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated.

### 13.3 Erasure

Where permitted by law, request deletion of certain personal data.

### 13.4 Withdraw consent

Where processing is consent-based, you may withdraw consent; withdrawal does not affect processing that already occurred lawfully before withdrawal.

### 13.5 Direct marketing

If we conduct direct marketing in future, we will seek consent as required by law; you may opt out at any time.

For access or correction requests, we may ask for information to verify your identity as permitted by law, and may charge a reasonable fee as allowed under the PDPO.
